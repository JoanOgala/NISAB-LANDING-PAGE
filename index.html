About NISAB 3.0

NISAB 2.0 is a student-focused training initiative organised by NIFES to help young people develop practical and profitable skills beyond the classroom

Participants will learn directly from experienced instructors through projects, workshops, assignment, and live practical lessons

Available Skill Tracks

Frontend Web Development

Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design, and modern web development practices.

Graphics Design

Master branding, social media design, typography, posters,and visual communication.

Video Editing

Learn cinematic editing, transitions, storytelling, and motion grapics.

UI/UX Design

Understand user experience, wireframming, prototyping, and interface design.

Mobile App Development

Build Android applications and cross-platform apps using modern tools.

Digital Marketing

Learn social media growth, content marketing, and audience trageting.
